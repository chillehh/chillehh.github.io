Project information

I've entered 6 Game Jams with my first being the Indie's Vs. PewDiePie game jam in 2014 when I was 14yrs old. Game Jams have been a fun way to challenge myself and my team in game development. There is a time constraint of 48-72hrs where you must create an entire game from scratch, which usually means blocking out an entire weekend to compete.

My most recent Game Jam entry was Ludum Dare 51: The Time Tree, created by myself and some work colleagues in Oct 2022. This was a game made in 72hrs and I believe is an impressive show of our skills considering. The game ended up coming 453rd overall out of 2000+ entries. It was overall a super fun experience, and I hope to enter another game jam in the near future :)
