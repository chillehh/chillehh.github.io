Chillehh is a YouTube channel I created during my tertiary studies to originally share my knowledge of a Binding of Isaac-like dungeon generation algorithm I had come up with in my own time. This became the most popular series on the channel and is still asked to this day for it to be continued.

I really enjoyed making the game/code behind each video I would make, and I used this channel as a way to gain skills in my communication. I believe because of this channel, I have built up a strong foundation of the ways to share programming knowledge as well as educating thousands of beginners start their journey in Unity 3D.
