About Me

As of 2021, I am in my third year of university, studying a Bachelor of IT (Digital Media). My first job involved helping make the game Experience, and I have 3 years experience teaching students to code during the school holidays at Code Camp. I enjoyed working Code Camp in the school holidays so much that I started up my own YouTube tutorial channel where I make tutorials in the game engine Unity, which allowed me to teach at a more advanced level with topics I am interested in. I am extremely passionate about 3D printing and have a keen interest in small businesses, which has led to the opening of my Etsy store where I design, print, and finish keycaps for mechanical keyboards.
