Experience - The App was a government funded mobile application with the purpose of educating children in domestic violence and bullying. The app was on the store from July 2017 - Jul 2019.

During 2016 and 2017 at my highschool, I was programming Experience as part of a team while employed at Revolition Games. The app was made using Unity 3D and was my first exposure to professional 3D game development.
